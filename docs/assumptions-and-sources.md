## Normalized Abstractions

- Armor values are relative protection bands, not millimeter equivalence.
- Training and awareness combine many human factors into deterministic modifiers.
- Fictional units are original archetypes with invented but bounded capability contracts.
- Projectile deflection, energy weapons, telekinesis, and powered armor flight bursts are explicitly fictional.

## Fictional Capability Contracts

- Dark Space Warlord: short-range energy blade, limited telekinetic throw cooldown, bounded projectile deflection, fear aura, vulnerable to concentrated explosives or heavy fire.
- Powered Armor Champion: high armor, finite energy blaster ammunition, finite micro-missiles, deterministic reposition bursts, no real-time flight physics authority.
- Combat Android: no morale, durable synthetic chassis, rifle accuracy, melee fallback, vulnerable to explosive and high-penetration damage.
